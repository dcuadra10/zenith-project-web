Mightiest Governor Event (MGE)

Fixed / Open MGEs

Pre-KvK / KvK 1: All Martel MGEs will be FIXED. All Caesar and El Cid MGEs will be OPEN.

After KvK 1: Most—if not all—MGEs will be FIXED unless there is a KvK fight that lands on day 5 or 6 (Kill Event) of MGE. If the MGE Kill Event aligns with a major KvK battle, the MGE will likely be OPEN/FFA.

What Is A Fixed MGE?

A fixed MGE is an event where regulations are strictly enforced to ensure specific players receive the highest rewards without needing to push an excessive amount of points/resources. This maximizes kingdom efficiency.

What Is An Open MGE?

An open MGE is an event where NO regulations are set. It is essentially a Free-For-All (FFA) competition where anyone can compete for any spot.

How It Will Work

Any and all members are welcome to apply for a fixed MGE position. Fixed placements will be determined by:

  • VIP Level
  • Commander Progression
  • Troop Count
  • Kill Points / Deaths (During & After KvK 1)
  • Armor & Armaments (KvK 2+)
  • Speedups & Resources

T5 and high-power T4 players will have priority for the highest MGE positions. This strategy ensures our strongest players have the best commanders to effectively defend our kingdom in KvK.

IMPORTANT NOTE

Just because stronger players get priority does NOT mean you will not get a fixed position. It simply means top positions are very likely to be allocated to the strongest players to benefit the kingdom as a whole.
