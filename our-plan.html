📜 Our Plan

Phase 1: Kingdom Opening

When 4153 opens we will immediately make the alliances (if a r5 isn't available on of their r4s will make it) after the alliances are create, we will secure the top 6 alliances spot and rotate members for max credit efficiency so we can put the central fortress of each alliance the fastest possible. After we got the top 6 we will start making diplomacy and bringing all the natives alliances to us by merge or by burning them. After we have no other alliances than ours in the kingdom and we put the cf we will start establishing the power between our alliances so they can fit to our alliances plan, and we can start with the preparations for sanctums, altars, z2, shrines, z3, Lt and KvK 1.

Phase 2: Zone 2 Transition

When zone 2 opens all the top 6 alliances will get their corresponding passes and will start following the z2 territory plan made by the Territory Department we plan to have a 3/3/2/1 shrine distribution, but all the top 6 alliances will get a shrine for the monument's rewards. Just the top 6 alliances will enter z2 before z3 is open.

Phase 3: Zone 3 and Home Kingdom

When the last zone of home kingdom zone 3 opens just the 4 fighter alliances will put territory on zone 3 and the 2 support alliances will have a pass to zone 3 so their members can send marches for guardians, baular runs, gather rss and etc.
