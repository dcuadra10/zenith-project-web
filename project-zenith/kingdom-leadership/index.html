Kingdom Leadership Structure

We plan to have a very useful and good leadership structure already tested in many imperiums and strong kingdoms of Rise Of Kingdoms.
The Kingdom Leadership structure will consist of a series of councils, departments and teams.

The High Council

The high council will be the leaders of the 5 departments: Territory, Data, War, Diplomacy, and Migration.

The Low Council

We will have a low council that will be the leaders of the teams (sub-departments supervised by their respective father department leader and co-leader) and the co-leaders of the departments.
