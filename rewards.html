Rewards Distribution

Holy Sites / Passes

Swapping between alliances for rewards will NOT be permitted.

Project Zenith alliances will not accept any players during the times of: 8 hours before holy sites are claimed and 30 minutes after holy sites are claimed.

Only exception: If a Project Zenith alliance needs help capturing a pass/site, 1-3 players WITH PERMISSION FROM LEADERSHIP are allowed to help.

Lost Temple Policy:

Rewards will be contribution-based (Tech donations, bank donations, help button, rallying forts, etc.).

Kingdom Trophies

Kingdom trophies will be distributed based on contribution to the kingdom. Actions that classify as contribution include:

  • Donating to Alliance Tech
  • Donating to the Kingdom Bank
  • Spamming the Help Button
  • Rallying Barbarian Fortresses
  • Actively participating in KvK

KvK Rewards

Passes & Holy Sites (Excluding Ziggurat):
Will use the same system as home kingdom passes/holy sites. Members will stay in their respective alliances and will not be permitted to alliance hop for rewards.

Ziggurat Rewards

Ziggurat rewards will be DKP and Honor based. Those who earn the most DKP and Honor will receive Ziggurat rewards.
